## Domain Number Note

Domain numbers are **permanent identifiers**, not pillar-sequential positions. Domain 06 (Education) belongs to Pillar II even though its number falls within the Pillar I range. The numbers come from the original domain assignment; the pillar grouping is the conceptual architecture. Both are stable — neither changes.

The civilization-building sequence follows the pillar order:
```
Pillar I  → 01, 02, 03, 04, 05, 07
Pillar II → 06, 12, 13
Pillar III → 14, 19, 09, 08, 15, 10
Pillar IV → 16, 17, 18, 20
Pillar V  → 11, 21
```
