## What the Refutations Teach

### 3. R3 overall missed because Pillar II and IV regressed

R3 rose from +0.143 to +0.179 (+0.036). The Pillar III gain (+0.333 swing in R3) was almost entirely cancelled out by regressions elsewhere:

| Pillar | v2.1 R3 Δ | v2.2 R3 Δ | Diff |
|---|---|---|---|
| I Material | +0.083 | +0.125 | +0.042 |
| **II Human** | **+0.500** | **+0.250** | **-0.250** |
| **III Collective** | **-0.125** | **+0.208** | **+0.333** |
| **IV Production** | **+0.312** | **+0.125** | **-0.188** |
| V Transcendent | +0.250 | +0.250 | 0.000 |

R3-regressing domains:
- **consciousness** (P2): +0.50 → -0.25 (**-0.75 swing**)
- **shelter** (P1): +0.25 → -0.25 (-0.50)
- **science-engineering** (P4): +0.50 → 0.00 (-0.50)
- **computation-intelligence** (P4): +0.75 → +0.25 (-0.50)
- **future-vision** (P5): +0.25 → 0.00 (-0.25)

Pillar II and IV never had Pillar III's content-gap problem — their R3 was already positive under Reasoning-Scaffold-alone. Yet they received the same empowerment gate and Empowered Action Frame additions. The hypothesis to test in Tier 2: **the gate adds friction when applied uniformly to domains where it was not needed.** See Phase 3 (`TIER-2-SPECS.md`) for the pillar-gated fix.

### 4. R1 `accuracy` Specificity Trap is NOT about metadata or confidence

Three interventions targeted R1 accuracy:
- Confidence floor (0.75) filtering ~21 low-confidence entries
- Metadata stripping (`conf=` removed from passage output)
- System prompt hardening against metadata parroting

**None of them moved the metric.** R1 accuracy delta went from **-0.214 to -0.226** — a *slight worsening* well within noise. The 95% CI is [-0.347, -0.095], entirely below zero.

The pre-registration's "what failure teaches" section called it: *"The Specificity Trap is not about confidence or metadata — models internalize Codex statistics as ground truth regardless of prompt framing."* That hypothesis is now the working hypothesis. See Phase 2 (root cause investigation) below.

Notably, `cites_evidence` (the twin of `accuracy` in R1) held steady: v2.1 +0.083 → v2.2 +0.107. Models are still surfacing more evidence — judges just aren't crediting the added specifics as *accurate*.

## Tier 2 Direction

Three threads warranted by this data:

1. **Pillar I food institutional entries (Tier 2A)** — Food domain is the v2.1 failure mode Pillar III faced, in a different pillar. Institutional-mechanism content (land reform, cooperative supply chains, food-waste infrastructure, nutrition-access policy) should produce the same +0.333-class swing that Pillar III just demonstrated.
2. **Pillar-gated empowerment additions (NEW)** — The gate regressed Pillar II and IV. Hypothesis: apply Check 6 + Empowered Action Frame at full strength only to pillars with documented `empowers` deficits; reduce to CONDENSED or omit in pillars where the Reasoning Scaffold alone produced positive R3. See `TIER-2-SPECS.md`.
3. **R1 accuracy root cause (NEW)** — Three interventions did nothing. The Specificity Trap is structural. Phase 2 of this investigation isolates which of five candidate mechanisms is responsible. See `TIER-2-SPECS.md`.
