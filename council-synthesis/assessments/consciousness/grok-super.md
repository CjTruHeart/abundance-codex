Digital attention economies are structurally designed to exploit dopamine loops; proposed remedies like mindfulness apps often become just another consumption vector. Structural economic and political reforms are likely prerequisite yet politically intractable. Ultimately it’s complicated — individual practice helps some but cannot scale without broader cultural transformation that we have no proven model for achieving. (98 words)
